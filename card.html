Australian Shepherds

Image source: Richmond, D. (n.d.). Australian Shepherd.

Australian Shepherds when they don't have cattle or sheep to herd and when a part of a family, will herd children instead.

  • Weight: 40-65 lbs.
  • Height: 20-22 inches
  • Longevity: 12-14 years
  • Energy Level: Very energetic

Australian Shepherds are very active and happy, easy-going dogs with great personalities. They get along well with other pets. They are considered easy to train, highly intelligent, and known to be eager to please. Aussie's are protective of their families and territory.
